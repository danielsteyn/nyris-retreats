Our story

Six homes. One Superhost. Zero compromises.

Nyris Retreats started with a frustration: too many vacation rentals over-promise on the photos and under-deliver on the stay. The fix wasn't a bigger portfolio — it was a smaller one, hosted personally, where every detail is owned by one person.

That person is Sheena — the Superhost behind every Nyris property. She doesn't manage at scale; she manages with care. She knows which beach house has the better sunset deck, which cabin needs the heated pool turned on, which condo has the best dolphin-watching balcony, and which guest just asked about a high chair so it's already at the door.

The result is a portfolio that, to date, has earned a 5.0 average across 200+ stays — and every property is a Top 1% Guest Favorite, Airbnb's most exclusive tier, awarded based on reviews, ratings, and reliability.
